Break Blocks

Game's code

Hello , in this game ypu have to break 100 blocks for level whit only 5 lifes .

This simbol means you are playing at desktop:

If you want move the Player's bar to left press left on you keyboard . If you want move the Player's bar to right press right on you keyboard. If you want start game press enter on your keyboard .

This simbol means you are playing on mobile :

If you want move the Player's bar to left press left button on your window . If you want move the Player's bar to right press right button on your window. If you want start game bar button enter on your window .

For now , this game only have five levels . Soon , it will have ten levels .

Thanks for playing .
